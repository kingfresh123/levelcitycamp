Level City Camp Leads First Ever Peace Walk And Cleanup In Boli Community

Peace Walk History – Boli Level City Camp (08 June 2025) On 8th June 2025, the peaceful community of Boli in the Upper West Region witnessed a remarkable event as the Level City Camp organized a powerful Peace Walk aimed at promoting unity, love, and development. The event brought together youth, community leaders, and residents from Boli and surrounding communities. Dressed in branded shirts and holding placards with messages of peace and togetherness, participants marched through the principal streets of Boli to spread awareness about peaceful coexistence and community growth. ”

The Peace Walk was strongly supported by key individuals who played vital roles in its success. Among them was DJ 10Plus, who energized the crowd with inspiring music and sound coordination throughout the procession. Also present was Toppie Junior, whose encouragement and presence motivated the youth to actively participate. A special appreciation goes to Boora, widely known in the community as “The Ever Supportive,” for his commitment and logistical support toward the event’s organization. The Peace Walk also received notable backing from the CEO of Xpress GH Online, a multiple award-winning blogger and strong advocate for growth and development in the Upper West Region. His involvement highlighted the importance of media support in promoting positive community initiatives and youth empowerment. The event concluded with a short gathering where speakers emphasized the need for unity, respect, and collective responsibility in building a peaceful and progressive Upper West Region. The Peace Walk stands as a historic moment for Boli Level City Camp, reinforcing the message that peace is the foundation for development. Theme: “Unity in Peace, Strength in Development.
